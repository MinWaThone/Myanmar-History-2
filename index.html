The history of Burma is a complex and fascinating tale of different cultures and civilizations. From the ancient Pyu city-states to the British colonial period, Burma has been shaped by its interactions with different empires and civilizations. One of the earliest kingdoms in Burma, the Pyu city-states, were established in the Irrawaddy valley around the 2nd century BC. Burma continued to develop as a kingdom throughout history, with periods of conflict and stability depending on the ruling dynasty.
